Benefits of Sports

Physical activity boosts your mood, strengthens your body, and improves overall health.
Need an emotional lift? Or need to lower stress after a stressful day? A gym session or brisk walk can help. Physical activity stimulates many brain chemicals that may leave you feeling happier, more relaxed and less anxious.

What are benefits of doing sports?

  • Makes your body strong and fit
  • Helps you feel happy and less stressed
  • Good for your heart and muscles

Do we always have to do sports?

  • No, but moving your body is important
  • You can walk, dance, or play instead
  • Just try to stay active often

Why are sports good for our health?

  • Help your body work better
  • Keep your heart, lungs, and muscles strong
  • Stop you from getting sick easily
